Who are we?

Renno & Co. is a law firm that concentrates on startup and emerging technologies law while offering ancillary business services to reduce coordination cost on entrepreneurs and investors.

If you are a venture capitalist, investor, incubator or accelerator, we provide you with tailor-made services and counsel to ensure the protection of your rights and funds. Our in-house emerging tech team can supply strategic consulting about your industry, allowing you to make more informed decisions.

If you are a startup, an entrepreneur, or have a bloody great idea, we can help propel your projects by providing all the services a company needs to get started and begin scaling.
